Warning: The MGED Ontology is deprecated and its concepts subsumed into the Ontology for Biomedical Investigation (OBI). Users are encouraged to migrate to OBI for updated content. MGED Ontology contains concepts, definitions, terms, and resources for standardized description of a microarray experiment in support of MAGE v.1. The MGED ontology is divided into the MGED Core ontology, intended to be stable and in synch with MAGE v.1, and the MGED Extended ontology, which adds further associations and classes not found in MAGE v.1.

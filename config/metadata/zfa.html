Zebrafish Model Organism Database (Zebrafish) is an ontology of zebrafish anatomical terms, including many definitions and synonyms. The ontology provides standard terminology for annotating gene expression and phenotypes, thus providing a link between these two types of data commonly used to study gene function. It is produced by the Zebrafish Information Network (ZFIN).
